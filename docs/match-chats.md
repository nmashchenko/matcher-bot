# 48-Hour Match Chats

## How It Works

After a mutual match, the bot creates a private two-person chat (plus the bot). The chat always closes and is deleted after exactly 48 hours — no exceptions.

This creates tempo and prevents dead dialogues from piling up.

## Bot as Host (2-4 Messages)

The bot starts the conversation to avoid the "hi how are you" trap:

1. "Yo. I matched you. I'm just here to kick things off so you don't drown in 'how are you.'"
2. "Each of you: in one sentence, what's your ideal evening in this city?"
3. "Now one by one: name one thing you genuinely like (food/music/hobby)."
4. "Okay, I see overlap in [X]. You're on your own now. I'm silent."

After that, users talk on their own until the 48-hour window closes.

## Why 48 Hours

- Creates urgency — conversations actually happen instead of rotting in a "matched" list.
- Prevents dead dialogues from piling up.
- If it went well, users already exchanged contacts within 48 hours.
- If it didn't, neither side is stuck in an awkward ghost situation.
